History

The MBTI questionnaire, first published in 1943, was originally developed in the United States by Katharine Cook Briggs and her daughter Isabel Briggs Myers.

Inspiration from carl Jung Carl Jung was a renowned Swiss psychiatrist. His theory of psychological types proposes that people are innately different, both in terms of the way they see the world and take in information, and how they make decisions.
